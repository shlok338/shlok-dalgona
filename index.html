☕ Dalgona Coffee Recipe ☕

This yummy South Korean coffee treat that's all over the internet only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard – you feel like you’ve won the jackpot, right?

Ingredients

  • Instant coffe
  • Sugar
  • Hot Water
  • Milk

Making Process

1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water:
2 tablespoons (tbsp) of instant coffee, 2 tbsp of sugar, and 2 tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl
2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.
3: Serve with Milk:
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw, and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
